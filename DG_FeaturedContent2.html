Game of the Month: Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play platform fighting game developed and published by Blue Mammoth Games. It features various game modes, including online multiplayer battles, local multiplayer, and a single-player campaign. With a diverse roster of characters and fast-paced gameplay, Brawlhalla offers an exciting experience for both casual and competitive players.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a game where players load into a map and jump from a battle bus into war and try to be the last to survive the storm which and other player which both deal damage. Players objectives are to "loot" building and chest that appear on the map to obtain guns and survive longer than your enemy that loaded into the same lobby as you. The player who can eliminate opponents and strategically use their shield and bandages to escape the storm wins in the end.

Madden 2024

Madden 2024 is a football video game developed by EA Tiburon and published by EA Sports. It is the latest installment in the long-running Madden NFL series, featuring updated graphics, gameplay improvements, and new features. With realistic player animations and strategic gameplay, Madden 2024 offers an immersive football experience for fans of the sport.
